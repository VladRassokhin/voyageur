0. Create a new database, schemas are available here:
	${VHOST_ROOT}/${PN}/ttrss_schema_(mysql|pgsql).sql

1. Adapt ${VHOST_ROOT}/${PN}/config.php to your setup:
	* database name, user, password, connection type
		(leave DB_HOST empty if you want to connect via unix socket)
	* single or multi user mode
